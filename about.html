About Me

Hello everyone! I'm Maddie Forbes. I'm a California girl living in a Utah world. For as long as I can remember I have loved everything art and design. For the longest time, I thought I wanted to be a painter, but I realized I was not just an artist, but a designer. It's just what I've always loved to do.

Nowadays, I am a senior studying web design living in Utah with my amazing husband and our cat. Life is great! I'm excited to finally start designing for others and do what I've always wanted to do.
